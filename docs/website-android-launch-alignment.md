# FI-YOU Website Android Launch Alignment

Last updated: 2026-06-17

## Android App Launch Support Scope

The official website should support the Flutter Android official release as a brand, trust, and policy guidance channel.

P0 maintenance items:

- Explain FI-YOU as an AI-powered self-discovery platform.
- Keep the non-diagnostic notice visible in product copy and legal pages.
- Describe U-Map, Diary, and Signature as flows based on the user's current records.
- Keep Android launch CTAs focused on waitlist or Play Store connection, not web checkout.
- Keep Terms, Privacy Policy, Disclaimer, and Refund Policy accessible from the footer.
- Preserve the dark, violet, and mint FI-YOU brand tone without implying clinical authority.

P1 maintenance items:

- Add real Android app screenshots after Product QA approval.
- Add FAQ entries for Android availability, data handling, U-Map limits, and non-diagnostic use.
- Replace the pending Play Store CTA with the real Play Store URL after publication.
- Expand SEO metadata with final app name, canonical URL, and share image.

## Copy Conflict Watchlist

Current website changes remove pricing-first and web-payment-first navigation. Product QA should still review the wider repository for possible app or policy conflicts:

- Avoid `Human Map`; use `U-Map`.
- Avoid claims like accurate personality analysis, diagnosis, treatment, counseling, or compatibility judgment.
- Avoid presenting U-Map or Signature as a fixed identity label.
- Avoid selling questions, reports, or Star packs before the Android app launch flow is ready.
- Avoid relationship-based copy that sounds like judging another person.
- Treat refund copy as a pre-launch draft until app marketplace policy and product structure are final.

Before Play Store publication:

- Header CTA: Android launch guidance.
- Hero CTA: Android waitlist intent.
- Final CTA: waitlist email and pending Play Store link.
- Footer: Android launch, legal docs, support contact.

After Play Store publication:

- Replace pending CTA with the live Play Store URL.
- Add `rel="noopener noreferrer"` if the link opens externally.
- Add Play Store badge image only after brand asset approval.
- Update SEO title and description to mention availability on Android.
- Add screenshot section with approved app screenshots.
- Confirm Privacy, Terms, Disclaimer, and Refund links are visible near download CTAs.
